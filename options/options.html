Your data

What stays here, and what goes to Notion

  • While you writeYour note text, selected text, and any attached page title and URL are autosaved in Chrome on this device so you can recover your draft.
  • When you choose SaveYour reviewed capture is sent to the Notion workspace and destination you selected.
  • Until it is safeQueued notes stay on this device until delivery succeeds or you delete them. Inactive drafts and delivered history are removed after 30 days.
  • In IncognitoDrafts and local history last only for that Incognito session. Saving still sends the capture to your selected Notion workspace.
  • When you use AIYour note is processed by Chrome’s on-device model only after you choose an AI action. Generated text is not added until you review and apply it.

Quick Note reads page details only when you open it. It does not continuously monitor browsing, run analytics, or show ads.
